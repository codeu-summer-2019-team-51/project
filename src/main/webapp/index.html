Read.Sleep.Repeat

We created this web application as part of Google CodeU program. We chose this project to spread our love of books and allow any of you to read and write reviews for books, save books to your bookshelf and discuss the books you love (or hate) with others in the forum.

We hope this web application helps you find books you love and people who love the same books you do! If you have any feedback, feel free to let us know here.
